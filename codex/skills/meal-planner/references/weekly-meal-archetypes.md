# Weekly Meal Archetypes

Use these archetypes as the default dinner model for Cuisine planning. The goal is not to repeat the exact same recipes every week, but to keep the same kind of meal on the same day.

For the default Cuisine week, also include lunches on Wednesday, Saturday, and Sunday. Do not let Wednesday be only a lunch day: Wednesday dinner must be present and should stay simple.

## Dinner Archetypes

| Day | Target genre | Example direction |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Monday | Creamy pasta or comforting pasta with a green vegetable mixed in | Coquillettes cremeuses au fromage with broccoli or peas |
| Tuesday | Grain risotto / pilaf / one-pot grain bowl with mushrooms or seasonal roasted vegetables | Petit epeautre, bulgur, or barley risotto with mushrooms and roasted cabbage |
| Wednesday | Very simple, easy, comforting family meal | Quenelles with mild tomato sauce and steamed carrots |
| Thursday | Family gratin or oven dish built around vegetables, potatoes or greens, and mild cheese | Potato-spinach-goat cheese gratin, respecting project feedback for potato prep |
| Friday | Eggs plus vegetables, preferably a complete but not heavy dish | Frittata with cherry tomatoes and roasted cauliflower |
| Saturday | Homemade pizza, flatbread, savory tart, or convivial build-at-table meal | Homemade vegetable pizza with tomato, mozzarella, mushrooms, zucchini, optional olives apart |
| Sunday | Soup, veloute, or light comfort meal plus tartines/toasts | Courgette-pea-feta veloute with gratineed tartines |

## Lunch Archetypes

| Day | Target genre |
| --- | --- |
| Wednesday lunch | Very quick lunch, reheatable or easy to assemble |
| Saturday lunch | Simple lunch, flexible around family schedule |
| Sunday lunch | Light lunch, not too much cooking before Sunday dinner |

## Adaptation Rules

- Keep the same genre for each dinner day unless the user asks otherwise.
- Vary the exact vegetables, cheese, grains, and seasoning according to season and recent meals.
- Prefer seasonal vegetables.
- Keep child-friendliness high: mild flavors, familiar textures, optional toppings separated when helpful.
- The week as a whole must be balanced; each individual meal may be simpler or more comfort-oriented.
- Simplicity matters, especially Wednesday.
- If the user rejects a generated plan, replace requested meals with simpler or closer-to-archetype options before changing the whole week.
