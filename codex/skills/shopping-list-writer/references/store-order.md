# Store Order

Use this default store route for Cuisine shopping lists.

## Section Order

1. Fruits et légumes
2. Boulangerie
3. Épicerie salée
4. Surgelé
5. Frais
6. Basiques et épices
7. À vérifier

## Sub-Order

### Fruits et légumes

1. Fresh herbs
2. Salad and leafy greens
3. Fresh vegetables
4. Potatoes and sweet potatoes
5. Fresh fruit
6. Citrus

Examples: basilic, salade, brocoli, courgettes, champignons, pommes de terre, citron.

### Boulangerie

1. Bread
2. Pizza dough and flatbreads when usually bought from the bakery section
3. Tortillas and wraps only if the store stocks them near bread

Examples: pain de campagne, pain complet, pâte à pizza.

### Épicerie salée

1. Pasta, rice, grains, and dry cereals
2. Canned and jarred vegetables
3. Tomato sauces and passata
4. Legumes
5. Oils, vinegars, and condiments
6. Seeds and nuts
7. Shelf-stable wraps, tortillas, gnocchi, and pizza bases when not fresh

Examples: pâtes, petit épeautre, passata, maïs, huile d'olive, graines de tournesol.

### Surgelé

1. Frozen vegetables
2. Frozen herbs
3. Frozen prepared vegetarian items

Examples: petits pois surgelés, épinards hachés surgelés.

### Frais

1. Eggs
2. Cream, milk, yogurt, and butter
3. Cheese
4. Fresh prepared pasta, gnocchi, and dough when refrigerated
5. Plant proteins and tofu

Examples: œufs, crème fraîche, yaourt, mozzarella, cheddar, feta, gnocchis frais.

### Basiques et épices

1. Salt and pepper
2. Dried herbs and spices
3. Flour, semolina, and baking basics
4. Pantry basics likely already at home

Examples: sel, poivre, paprika, muscade, thym séché, farine.

### À vérifier

Use this section only when classification is unclear or when the recipe quantity is too vague to shop confidently.
